Exercises

It is important to get a workout in order to maintain healthy and keep your weight steady. It also helps to reduce any further health risks and improve your strength, ability to do things, and brain health. You must start out slowly and remember to stay hydrated.

According to CDC , physical health can bring immense health benefits to everyone. It helps to lose weight, reduce risks for heart disease and stroke which are "two leading causes of death in the US". Being active also mean enhancing your body functions and mood and keeping blood sugar levels in control.

The goal is to exercise for a total of 150 minutes of moderate exercise. Try to incorporate 30 minutes of physical activity each day for 5 days a week. Break down the workout for 10 minutes each if you need. Here are some suggested exercises for you to get started! Each one has a timer of 30 seconds so you can keep track.
